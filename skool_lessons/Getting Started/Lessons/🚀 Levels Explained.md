### **⚡️ LEVEL UP + EARN REWARDS ⚡️**

Here's how to rise through the ranks, unlock real rewards, and watch your profile title evolve as you level up 👇

**🎮 How Points Work**

Every action within the community earns XP (points):

👍 Like received — +1

💬 Comment — +1

🪄 Post that gets engagement — +3–5

🎓 Complete a lesson — +1

❤️ Helping others — bonus XP manually awarded

At each level, your title automatically changes on your profile and beside your name across the community. Everyone can see your progress.
