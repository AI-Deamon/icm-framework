**A few tools to help visual design**

Just stumbled on these and wanted to share:

A google chrome extension that creates a [DESIGN.md](http://DESIGN.md "http://DESIGN.md") of the website, super easy to just get the visual style of the website to use in CLaude:

[https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/designmd-style-extractor/ogpdnchdjiibhobphelbbkemnnemkfma](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/designmd-style-extractor/ogpdnchdjiibhobphelbbkemnnemkfma "https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/designmd-style-extractor/ogpdnchdjiibhobphelbbkemnnemkfma")

the repo: [https://github.com/bergside/design-md-chrome](https://github.com/bergside/design-md-chrome "https://github.com/bergside/design-md-chrome")
